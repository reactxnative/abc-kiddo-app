# Privacy Policy for ABC Kiddo

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026

**ReactXNative** ("we," "our," or "us") operates the **ABC Kiddo** mobile application (the "App"). We are fully committed to protecting the privacy of our young users and providing a safe, educational environment. 

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within the App. ABC Kiddo is designed strictly as a family-safe educational application.

### 1. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
ABC Kiddo is designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, request, or store any personal information or data from children, satisfying the requirements of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). 

### 2. Information Collection and Use
* **No Personal Data Collected:** The App does not require user registration, profiles, accounts, or logins. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifier.
* **No Device Trackers or Persistent Identifiers:** In strict compliance with Google Play's Families Policy, the App does not request, transmit, or utilize persistent hardware identifiers or advertising IDs (such as AAID/Ad ID, MAC addresses, or IMEIs).
* **No Location Data:** The App does not access, collect, or transmit any precise or approximate device location data.

### 3. Local Data Storage
Any progression metrics (such as completed tracing tasks or milestones) are stored **locally on the user's device** using isolated secure application memory. This information never leaves the device and is completely wiped out if the App is uninstalled.

### 4. Third-Party Services & SDKs
We do not integrate any third-party social media tools, third-party analytics platforms, or external SDKs that monitor behavior. All core components of the application run natively and securely within the user's local hardware container.

### 5. Advertisements and In-App Purchases
ABC Kiddo is a clean, non-commercial environment:
* There are **no advertisements** displayed inside the App.
* There are **no hidden in-app purchases** or micro-transactions.

### 6. Security
We prioritize the absolute security of your app experience. Because the App operates entirely locally and does not transmit data over the internet, your child's data is fundamentally isolated from remote web security breaches.

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to maintain compliance with Google Play developer ecosystem revisions. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
